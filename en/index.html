Redemption and Connection to God

Exploring the path to spiritual fulfillment and divine connection

About Geulah

Geulah (גאולה) is a Hebrew term meaning "redemption" or "salvation." It represents the concept of spiritual liberation and the restoration of a deep connection with the divine. This website explores the multifaceted nature of redemption and offers insights into how we can connect more deeply with God in our daily lives.

The journey of redemption is both personal and collective. It involves healing, transformation, and the realization of our highest spiritual potential. Through understanding the principles of Geulah, we can navigate our path toward greater meaning, purpose, and divine connection.

The Beginning of Geulah

By revealing that Geulah of Israel began (beginning of the calendar year 1998), God revealed that it will not spread to all the people at the same time. The aim of God is to first prepare a small herd of pioneers responding to his call. These pioneers have been named first-fruits and are expected to be columns of Geulah of Israel. In this phase we find ourselves today.

The publication of these first fruits on the tree of Israel is one of the most tangible evidence of the beginning of redemption, the buds of the heavenly rain that is about to fall on all the people in abundance.

The progress of redemption is therefore linked to the personal response of each individual to the call of God. The more people respond positively to this call, the faster the redemption will spread throughout Israel.

Dimensions of Redemption

Personal Redemption

At the individual level, Geulah involves the liberation of the soul from limiting beliefs, negative patterns, and spiritual confusion. It's a journey of self-discovery, healing, and alignment with our higher purpose.

Collective Redemption

Beyond the individual, Geulah encompasses the healing and transformation of communities, nations, and ultimately, all of humanity. It represents the vision of a world united in peace, justice, and spiritual awareness.

Global Redemption

In its most expansive sense, Geulah extends to the healing of our planet and the restoration of harmony between humanity and nature. It envisions a world where all life is recognized as sacred and interconnected.

Begin Your Journey of Redemption

The path of redemption and divine connection is available to everyone. It begins with a sincere desire to know God and live in alignment with divine principles.
