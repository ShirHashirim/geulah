Pioneers of redemption in our time

The Beginning of Geulah

By revealing that Geulah of Israel began (beginning of the calendar year 1998), God revealed that it will not spread to all the people at the same time. The aim of God is to first prepare a small herd of pioneers responding to his call. These pioneers have been named first-fruits and are expected to be columns of Geulah of Israel. In this phase we find ourselves today.

The publication of these first-fruits of the tree in Israel is one of the most tangible evidence of the beginning of Geulah, the harbinger that the celestial rain will be poured abundantly on all the people.

Geulah advance is thus linked to the progress of first-fruits. The difficulties experienced by Israel today are the birth pangs of Geulah, namely the birth of his first-fruits.

Over the Geulah progressing within the people-through to his first-fruits of all evil and corruption hidden exteriorized, particularly in structures of the country. When God will judge that the entire flock of first-fruits, then the Geulah be extended to all Israel, in a way that only he knows.

The Role of First Fruits

Preparation and Purification

The first-fruits of Geulah undergo an intensive process of preparation and purification. They are called to a deeper level of spiritual awareness and commitment, as they will serve as guides and examples for others when the full redemption comes.

This preparation involves letting go of old patterns, healing from past wounds, and aligning more fully with divine will. It is a challenging but transformative process that prepares them for their role in the broader redemption.

Bearing Witness

The first-fruits bear witness to the reality of Geulah in their own lives. Through their transformation and the fruits of their spiritual journey, they demonstrate the possibility and the path of redemption for others.

Their testimony is not primarily in words but in the quality of their being and the nature of their presence. They embody the values and consciousness of the redeemed life, serving as living examples of what is possible.

Building Bridges

The first-fruits serve as bridges between the old consciousness and the new, between the world as it has been and the world as it is becoming. They help others navigate the transition to a more spiritually aligned way of being.

This bridge-building function is essential for the smooth unfolding of the redemptive process, as it helps minimize resistance and confusion during times of profound change.
